Kindred who are newly embraced and the lowest rung of the [[Tremere Chantry]] taught by the [[Upper Apprentice(s) of the chantry]]. They become [[Lower Apprentice(s) of the Chantry]] once they are released and considered truly part of the clan. They are often embraced by the [[Tremere Magister]] or [[Upper Apprentice(s) of the chantry]] with special permission from the [[Seneschal]]
